About Us

Who We Are

We are a group of students passionate about simplifying the college selection process. Our team is working on creating a chatbot that provides 12th-grade students with quick and accurate information about engineering colleges in Gujarat, including cutoffs, opening and closing ranks, and campus details. We aim to make college research easier and less time-consuming for students.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create an intuitive chatbot that provides 12th-grade students with real-time, accurate information about engineering colleges in Gujarat, including cutoffs, ranks, and locations. We aim to simplify the college selection process by making this data easily accessible, helping students make informed decisions quickly and efficiently. Our goal is to save time, reduce stress, and streamline the college research process for every student.
